ABOUT

THE ARCHIVE

The Cyberpunk Archive was founded in 2019 as a preservation project for the rapidly expanding genre of cyberpunk art, music, and culture. What began as a personal collection has grown into a living repository maintained by a global community of operatives.

We believe that culture should be free and accessible. The archive is non-commercial, community-driven, and will remain online as long as there are people willing to maintain it. Contribute to the archive and join our mission to document the digital underground.

MISSION

In a world increasingly dominated by corporate content ecosystems, we believe in preserving the raw, unfiltered output of the underground. No algorithms. No engagement metrics. Just content.
